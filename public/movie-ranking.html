Best movies of all time according to Gemini

The top 3 movies of all time are:

1.Citizen Kane (1941):

This Orson Welles masterpiece is renowned for its innovative cinematography, complex narrative, and exploration of themes like power, ambition, and the American Dream. The film's iconic deep-focus shots and nonlinear storytelling have had a profound influence on subsequent filmmakers.

2.The Godfather (1972):

Francis Ford Coppola's epic crime drama is celebrated for its rich characters, intricate plot, and unflinching portrayal of the Corleone family's rise to power. The film's iconic themes of family, loyalty, and corruption have made it a timeless classic.

3.2001: A Space Odyssey (1968):

Stanley Kubrick's science fiction masterpiece is known for its visually stunning imagery, philosophical themes, and enigmatic narrative. The film's exploration of human evolution, artificial intelligence, and the nature of existence has captivated audiences for decades.
